### What are Runewords?

Like gold, blue, or white items, Runewords are a type of item in Diablo II. However, these items cannot be dropped by monsters but must be crafted by placing specific runes into items with sockets.

Socket rule: Runewords are only activated when the correct number of runes are socketed into the correct number of sockets in the correct order. This means that a 4-socketed item cannot be socketed with a Runeword designed for 3 sockets.

Item rule: Depending on the type of Runeword, there will be specific item requirements such as swords, shields, armor, helmets, etc. Additionally, Runewords can only be activated with gray items (Non-Magical Items) of Normal, Exceptional, Elite, Low Quality, Superior, or Ethereal quality.

Order rule: the order of the runes socketed into the item must be correct from left to right as shown in the table below.
