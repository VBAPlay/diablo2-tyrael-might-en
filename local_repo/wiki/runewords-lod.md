### What are Runewords?

Just like gold, blue, or white items, Runewords are a type of item in Diablo II. However, these items cannot be dropped by monsters but must be crafted by inserting runes into items with sockets.

Socket rule: Runewords only activate when the correct number of runes are socketed into the correct number of sockets in the correct order. This means an item with 4 sockets cannot be socketed with a 3-socket Runeword.

Items rule: each type of Runeword has specific requirements for items such as swords, shields, armors, helmets, etc. Additionally, Runewords can only be activated on gray items (Non-Magical Items) of Normal, Exceptional, Elite, Low Quality, Superior, or Ethereal quality.

Order rule: the order of the runes socketed into the item must be accurate from left to right according to the table below.
